Patrick
Mahomes

Quarterback for the Kansas City Chiefs




Patrick Mahomes II was born on September 17, 1995 in Tyler, Texas. He played college football at Texas Tech University. His father was a pitcher in Major League Baseball. In 2017 he was selected 10th overall by the Kansas City Chiefs. Mahomes went of for an explosive first year starting, throwing for over 5000 yards and 50 touchdowns. Mahomes has lead the chiefs to 5 consecutive AFC championships, 3 super bowls and 2 super bowl victories. The future is very bright for this established NFL superstar.
